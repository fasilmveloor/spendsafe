SpendSafe helps you track your expenses, manage your accounts, and ensuring you stay within your daily safe spending pace.

Features:
- **Smart Dashboard**: Know your "Free to Spend" amount daily.
- **Transaction Tracking**: Easy expense logging.
- **Account Management**: Track bank accounts, wallets, and cash.
- **Data Privacy**: All data is stored locally.
- **Backup**: Optional backup to Google Drive or local export.
- **No Ads**: Clean, focused interface.
